Our purpose

The purpose of
"Seniorpreneurs" is to empower senior high school students
who have a passion for entrepreneurship and business ownership.
Our goal is to provide valuable guidance, resources, and support
to help young aspiring entrepreneurs navigate
the challenges of starting and running a business while still in high school.
We aim to inspire and equip seniorpreneurs
with the knowledge, skills, and confidence
needed to turn their business ideas into successful ventures.
By fostering innovation, creativity, and a strong entrepreneurial spirit,
we strive to cultivate the next generation of business leaders
who will make a positive impact in the world of entrepreneurship.
